How do I register an account?

  • If you have not already signed up, go to Sign Up page.
  • Put in your email and create your password.
  • Alternatively, you can sign up with your Facebook or Google+ account.
  • Choose your business line, it determines what type of account you will be registered as.

  • Fill up the details to tell about yourself and your bussiness.
    DROPBUG PRO TIP
    It is important to tell as much as you can about your product and bussiness in 'About' to make it easier for other users to find you.
  • Done!! You're set to go!
How to add a new product?

  • Once you have signed in, click on the My Products at the bottom bar
  • Click Add New Product under the My Product title.
  • Fill in the details of the product.
  • Use the description to add additional information about the product.
  • Custom pricing can be used if you sell your product by bundle.
  • You can also set a Minumum Order Quantity under Advance Option.
  • Click Add Product when you are done.
  • You have to hit refresh on My Products page to see the newly added product.
    • P/S : Only supplier and stockist can add product
How to link with other users?

  • Once you have signed in, click on the Connect at the bottom bar
  • View the lists of Suppliers, Stockists, and Dropship Agents.
  • To see more users in one of the category, click View More
  • Click on the name of the user that you are interested in.
  • To send a link request, click Add Supplier/Stockist/Dropship at the top of the user profile
  • Wait for the Supplier/Stockist/Dropship to approve your request.
  • Once approved, you will get an email notification and also a notification under "What's New? section at the Home page
    • DROPBUG PRO TIP
    To view your linked user, click on the My Linked User from the top left Navigation Bar
How do I order or request a product?

  • Click on your desired supplier/stockist for purchasing.
  • Click See Product at the bottom of the user profile
  • Click Request Product.
  • Fill in your order details and click Send Request.
    P/S : If you choose Delivery as the type of request, you have to fill in the delivery address.
  • During the process, you can always check your order status at the My Active Listing at the bottom bar.
  • Wait for the seller to approve your request. You will be notified when your order has been approved, via in app notification and email.
  • Once approved, you can pay as instructed by the seller by clicking on the Pay Now button
    DROPBUG PRO TIP
    Upload a Proof of Payment for verification after you click on the Pay Now. It is optional but by doing so you can build your credibility with your seller
  • Once your payment has been verified, you will receive an in app notification and an email.
  • Wait for the seller to deliver your package or instruction for pickup
    DROPBUG PRO TIP
    If you are the seller you also can upload a Delivery Comfirmation for verification after you click on the Deliver. It is optional but by doing so you can build your credibility with your buyer
  • Completed order will be recorded in your Transaction History from the left side navigation bar.
    P/S : For Delivery type of request, the transaction is considered to be complete after the payment and package receive is confirmed.
    For Pick Up type of request, the transaction is considered to be complete after the seller approve the request and the payment is confirmed.
How to add DropBug to my homescreen (phone/tablet)?

  • For iPhone/iOS, open Dropbug homepage in Safari and click
  • Select Add to Home Screen.
  • The DropBug web page icon should appear on your home screen.
  • For Android, open DropBug homepage, click the Settings button in your browser
  • Select Add to Home Screen.
  • The DropBug web page icon should appear on your home screen.
